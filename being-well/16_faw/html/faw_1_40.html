A credit rating (sometimes referred to as a credit score) is used by banks and other financial institutions to determine whether finance can be offered. Your credit rating (or credit score) will be taken into account when you apply for a mobile phone contract, personal loan or credit card.

Factors that will affect your credit score include:

  • The amount of money you have borrowed
  • The number of credit applications you have made, e.g. for credit cards, mobile phone contracts
  • Whether you pay on time.
