While hours of studying will burn lots of mental energy, did you know that both your mind and body need physical activity in order for you to perform at your best? Being at university can be one of the most exciting times of your life, but it will also make demands upon your health and wellbeing.

This module considers the benefits of physical activity and shows you how to build it appropriately into your student life.
