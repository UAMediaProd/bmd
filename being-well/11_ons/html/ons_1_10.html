Get Safe Online is a useful website providing practical advice on how to protect yourself, your computers and mobile devices against fraud, identity theft, viruses and many other problems encountered online.

When thinking about your everyday interaction online, it is important to keep in mind that what we say, post and share is often public or can be seen by others in a group.
