 It is important to think about how much time we are spending online and what we are doing in balance with other activities in our lives It is really important to think about using privacy settings, using secure sites to buy things, and using passwords to protect our information We should think carefully about how we use technology in our everyday relationships, especially intimate ones If you are worried about the way people behave toward you online, remember you can respond proactively by either blocking them or reporting the behaviour We should always be vigilant about any emails we receive, what links we click on, and any personal information we share Images that are posted or shared online without consent can be very upsetting, and in some cases, illegal. These should be reported It is not acceptable to hide behind freedom of speech when making hateful comments about protected characteristics. Such posts can be reported and in extreme cases someone could end up with a criminal record.
