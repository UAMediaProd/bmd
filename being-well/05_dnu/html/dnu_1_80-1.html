 Eating well means eating a wide variety of foods from all food groups Eat regularly: aim to have three main meals and a few snacks every day Keep hydrated by drinking plenty of water and avoid energy and soft drinks Eat plenty of vegetables, fruit, wholegrains, legumes, nuts and seeds Try not to overindulge in food with low nutritional value, such as highly processed 'fast' food If you are concerned about your eating attitudes, it's important to seek help from someone you trust Spend a few minutes each week to plan your meals and grocery shopping Learn to put together a few simple nutritious dishes and snacks, and enjoy eating with others! Our diet is associated with our wellbeing, so being well means eating well.
