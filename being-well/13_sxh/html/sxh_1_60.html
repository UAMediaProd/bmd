Managing sexual health can at times feel awkward, embarrassing and even stressful. For sexually-active adults however, it is something that needs to be considered, and will have huge benefits for your physical health, sense of wellbeing and relationships.

Keeping healthy helps you enjoy your sex life, and helps make sure your partner(s) enjoy it too. Use the tools available to you: safer sex supplies, contraception, STI testing, support services, and helpful friends to keep on top of your sexual wellbeing.
