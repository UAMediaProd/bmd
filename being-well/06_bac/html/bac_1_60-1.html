Action plans are used to set physical activity goals and to track your progress in achieving them. To become physically active, build different forms of vigorous, moderate and muscle-strengthening exercises into your weekly action plan.

Remember that adequate sleep, rest and relaxation goals are also needed for you to benefit from exercise. The five-step cycle can be used to provide a guideline for identifying, actioning and reviewing physical activity goals.
