Support services

HelpGuide is a non-profit mental health and wellness website:
https://www.helpguide.org/articles/relationships-communication/making-good-friends.htm

Relationships Australia

Relationships Australia is a leading provider of relationship support services for individuals, families and communities. It aims to support all people in Australia to achieve positive and respectful relationships.
https://www.relationships.org.au/relationship-advice/relationship-advice-sheets/

Lifeline

Lifeline is a free 24/7 counselling and crisis support service. Call: 13 11 14, text: 0477 13 11 14, or go online:
https://www.lifeline.org.au/

The Line or 1800 MYLINE

The Line is a counselling service for young people who are concerned about their own behaviour and want to make positive changes. Call: 1800 695 463, or go online :
https://www.theline.org.au/

1800 Respect

1800 Respect is a national sexual assault, family and domestic violence counselling line:
https://www.1800respect.org.au

Men's Referral Service

This is an anonymous and confidential telephone counselling, information and referrals service for boys and men to help them stop violent and controlling behaviour:
http://mrs.org.au/

MensLine

MensLine has resources that explore the challenging aspects of maintaining a healthy relationship:
https://www.mensline.org.au
